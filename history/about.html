The drum show "MARSHAL" leaves vivid impressions for a long time.

Playing drums, a musician hears music in his own way. For him, rhythm matters more than melody. But playing polyrhythmy, the conditions under which different sizes of musical rhythms intersect, something incomprehensible happens. It seems that the entire universe is revealing its secrets.

FORERUNNER

The main idea of ​​the Marshal project originated in 2014. All events preceding this project were reactive in nature. The youthful dream of a young musician drummer could have ended in a dream, if not for the events that took place in Ukraine at the specified time. Love for music revived youthful passions. Having reached adulthood and having acquired sufficient experience in organizational and financial activities, it was decided to create a commercial instrumental project in the form of a musical show. Having devoted sufficient time to perfecting the performing skills, it was later proposed to create a drum show that would include elements of theatrical choreography. A special role in this project is played by the performance of the vocal parts of the connacol.

BOOKS

Project Marshal is also a publishing activity. Books in electronic and printed form are available on various Internet sites and in retail chains. The author of books, talks about his experience as a musician and as a specialist in related fields: engineering, programming, design, etc. Shares his knowledge, gives advice to beginners and enters into polemics with experienced musicians. Having an outstanding teaching experience, the author expresses his thoughts and observations in an accessible form. On the pages of books, readers will be able to find a lot of useful advice and recommendations. By writing to the author by e-mail, the reader will be able to ask questions of interest or discuss publications. Coming soon, a new work "Mixed Drumming Technique", where the musician will acquaint readers with his new method of mixed drumming in contemporary music. The book will also touch upon the topic of the application of the connacle technique as an element of the performing arts. Connacol is a rendition of vocal stressed syllables in Indian carnatic music.

STUDIO

The Marshall project team is a group of enthusiasts who are in love with their work. It is also a multi-stream project covering many related areas and technologies: electronics, engineering, telemetry, design and computer-aided design. All this work is focused on the implementation of an ambitious project of a new generation musical theatrical show. Although our work is focused on the production of copyright content, nevertheless, we are open for collaboration and will be glad to cooperate with all interested musicians, artists and producers. We are also confident that together, we will be able to create a world-class music show. We believe "Show must go on!"
