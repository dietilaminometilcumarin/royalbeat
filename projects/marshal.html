MARSHAL - Music & Drum & Light

Instrumental and musical theater show ­M.A.R.S.H.A.L.

MARSHAL is an experimental musical project presented in the form of a theatrical drum show, where its author demonstrates a high level of performing skills, playing various types of percussion instruments using unique playing techniques. It is also a light show filled with a variety of visual effects.

- these are complex-compound drum rhythms - polyrhythm with elements of high-speed solos, built on the basis of parade diddles.

- this is electronic-acoustic music in the genre of progressive Jazz-Rock with an admixture of new synthesized sound and Vocodex effect.

The use of lighting equipment.

The colorfulness and uniqueness of the musical show is also achieved through the use of various lighting solutions. These include: laser devices, rotating heads, smoke machines, and more. All of them are safe for public use and do not have any suffocating or toxic effects on humans.

Sound design at the highest level.

The show does not use phonogram or fake music tracks. Only high-quality live stereo sound created by a high-class synthesizing system similar to sound modules and synthesizers. Light Head

Guaranteed safety and quality

Moving heads are lighting fixtures that direct a beam of light to the right place by rotating a part of their body horizontally and vertically. Moving heads belong to the category of smart devices or so-called "smart lights", that is, they are able to do things that are beyond the capabilities of traditional lighting devices.

Stroboscope

Guaranteed safety and quality

A stroboscope is a device that allows you to quickly reproduce repetitive bright light pulses. Equipped with a rotary mirror, the stroboscope is able to change the direction of light pulses according to a given program or manually.


Effects laser

Guaranteed safety and quality

A stage laser is a device that emits beams of powerful laser beams that penetrate the entire space and create bright, spectacular lighting effects during celebrations. These devices are capable of drawing in space all kinds of patterns and even continuous images.

Smoke machine

Guaranteed safety and quality

A smoke generator (smoke machine) is a device that produces smoke or mist and disperses it. The principle of operation of most smoke machines is as follows: a special liquid is supplied by a pump to a thermocouple, heats up, evaporates, and then exits through the nozzle of the atomizer. smoke is safe for human health as it contains: glycerin, water and alcohol.
